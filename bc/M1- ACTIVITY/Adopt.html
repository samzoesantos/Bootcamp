Welcome to Hero Hounds Sanctuary Volunteer Page!

At Hero Hounds Sanctuary, we are passionately dedicated to the well-being of retired police and service dogs. These canine heroes, having spent their lives in service, deserve a serene and loving environment in their golden years – and you can help make this happen!

Why Volunteer with Us?

  • Make a Difference: Be a part of a compassionate community that provides a nurturing retirement to these remarkable dogs.
  • Honor Their Service: These dogs have been pivotal in search and rescue missions, crime detection, and providing aid to those with disabilities. Volunteering with us means you are part of celebrating their bravery and commitment.
  • Connect with Canine Heroes: Experience the joy and satisfaction of interacting with these amazing dogs. Each one has a story to tell.
